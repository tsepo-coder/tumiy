Celebrating Culture & Tourism

This event showcases Lesotho’s rich traditions, music, dance, and breathtaking landscapes.

History

The country's natural beauty, with its mountains and valleys, has always been a draw. In 1990s tourism grew with initiatives like Lesotho Highlands Water project. Today Lesotho's tourism focuses on eco-tourism, cultural experiences and adventure activities like skiing, hiking and pony trekking.

  • Local Cuisine Tasting
